## Full Text

The conjecture treats invariants as fixed objects that the corpus either contains or lacks, but invariants are themselves theory-laden: what counts as the invariant of a domain depends on the conceptual framework brought to it. This raises the question of whether the inductive bias of a token-prediction architecture implicitly privileges certain invariant decompositions over others, and whether this bias is epistemically neutral or systematically distorts which invariants get extracted — particularly in domains where the 'correct' invariant is contested.
