## Full Text

How can 'knowledge-bearing invariant relations' be defined and measured non-circularly in token-prediction systems? The conjecture relies on internal representations encoding truth-relevant structure that survives rewording, recombination, criticism, and counterfactual transformation, but it leaves open a rigorous criterion distinguishing genuine structural representation from merely useful statistical compression. A key problem is to specify tests or formalisms that identify invariants without presupposing the very explanatory knowledge they are meant to detect.
