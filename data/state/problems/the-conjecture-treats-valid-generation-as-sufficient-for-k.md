## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'valid generation' as sufficient for knowledge-constitution, but validity is itself a judgment that requires a critic. If LLMs cannot reliably criticize their own outputs, can they reliably recognize validity in the first place? This creates a circularity: the conjecture grants LLMs knowledge-creation on the basis of a capacity (validity-detection) that the conjecture's own constraint (weak self-criticism) calls into question. Resolving this requires a cleaner account of whether generation and validation are separable epistemic acts.
