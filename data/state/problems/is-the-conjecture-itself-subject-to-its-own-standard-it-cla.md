## Full Text

Is the conjecture itself subject to its own standard? It claims that current AI systems cannot identify errors in frameworks that no prior text has flagged — but the conjecture was generated by reasoning about AI systems, not by direct empirical refutation of a prior theory. If the conjecture is a genuine novel conjecture, it either demonstrates that the system generating it has structural sufficiency (undermining the claim about current systems) or it was produced by retrieval-with-interpolation (in which case the conjecture's own epistemic status is unclear). This self-referential tension is worth making explicit.
