## Full Text

The conjecture introduces 'invariant signal to distributional noise ratio' as the critical variable but leaves it undefined. For logic and mathematics this ratio is claimed to be high, but this is asserted rather than derived. A productive open problem is to formalize this ratio — perhaps as a measure of how much mutual information the corpus instances share about the invariant relative to domain-irrelevant variation — and to determine whether it can be estimated from corpus statistics alone, prior to training.
