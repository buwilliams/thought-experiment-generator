## Full Text

The conjecture focuses on synchronic structure — the constraint relations among components at a given time. But explanations evolve: what begins as a swappable schema can become load-bearing as auxiliary commitments accumulate around it, and what begins as load-bearing can become swappable as the field finds alternative derivations of the same explanandum. Is there a dynamics of explanation structure, and does the conjecture's criterion track a stable property or a time-indexed one?
