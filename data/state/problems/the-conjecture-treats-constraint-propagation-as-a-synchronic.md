The conjecture treats constraint propagation as a synchronic structural property of a finished explanation. But explanations are constructed and revised over time. Is there a dynamic version of the criterion — one that tracks how constraint structure changes as an explanation develops — and would it predict which research programs are converging on real structure versus accumulating decorative detail?
