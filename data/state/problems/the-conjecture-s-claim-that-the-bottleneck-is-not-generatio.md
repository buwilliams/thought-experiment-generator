## Full Text

The conjecture's claim that 'the bottleneck is not generation but criticism' has a direct empirical implication: systems with stronger self-criticism (e.g., models trained with RLHF, constitutional AI, or debate-based feedback) should produce more cumulative knowledge-growth than base models with equivalent generative capacity. This is a testable prediction that the conjecture does not flag as such. Identifying and running this test would either confirm the structural diagnosis or reveal that the bottleneck is elsewhere.
