## Full Text

The conjecture implicitly treats human cognition as having the selection mechanism LLMs lack — a conjecture-and-refutation cycle. But human reasoning is also well-documented to produce fluent confabulation (confabulation in neuropsychology, motivated reasoning, post-hoc rationalization). If humans also lack reliable internal signals separating genuine explanation from fluent imposture, the contrast the conjecture draws may be one of degree rather than kind. Does this undermine or refine the argument?
