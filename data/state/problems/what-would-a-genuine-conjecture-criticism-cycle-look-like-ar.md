What would a genuine conjecture-criticism cycle look like architecturally in a learning system? The conjecture distinguishes 'rewarded output' from 'conjecture that survived criticism' but does not specify what mechanisms would instantiate the latter. This is the central open problem the conjecture raises and leaves entirely unaddressed. Is it a different loss function, a different training loop structure, an adversarial critic that is itself subject to revision, or something with no current analog? The gap between diagnosing the absence and specifying the presence is large and worth closing.
