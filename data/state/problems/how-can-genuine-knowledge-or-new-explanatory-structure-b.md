## Full Text

How can 'genuine knowledge' or 'new explanatory structure' be operationalized without collapsing back into proxy metrics like predictive accuracy, benchmark performance, or human plausibility judgments? The conjecture relies on a Popperian notion of explanation surviving criticism, but it remains open how to test for explanatory improvement, sensitivity to counterexample, and rival-comparison in a way that is rigorous and non-circular.
