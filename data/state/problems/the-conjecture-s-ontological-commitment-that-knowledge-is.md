## Full Text

The conjecture's ontological commitment — that knowledge is fundamental — creates an interesting tension with its own argument. If knowledge is not reducible to substrate, then the claim that 'more substrate amplifies the problem' requires a non-substrate account of what epistemic reach actually is and how it is instantiated. The conjecture borrows Deutschian epistemology but does not fully cash out the ontological implications for what a knowledge-generating system would need to look like physically.
