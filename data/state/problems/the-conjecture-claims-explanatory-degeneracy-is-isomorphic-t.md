## Full Text

The conjecture claims explanatory degeneracy is isomorphic to underdetermination, but underdetermination is an epistemic relation between theory and evidence, while degeneracy is defined purely over inferential structure. Is this isomorphism exact, approximate, or merely analogical? A precise mapping would need to show that every free variable in the evidential constraint system corresponds to a swappable node in the explanatory graph and vice versa. If the isomorphism fails in either direction, the conjecture's most ambitious claim collapses into a suggestive metaphor.
