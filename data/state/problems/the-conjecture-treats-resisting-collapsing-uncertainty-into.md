## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'resisting collapsing uncertainty into confident output' as a structural virtue. But in many deployment contexts, a system that refuses to collapse uncertainty is not useful — action requires commitment. There is an unresolved tension between epistemic virtue (maintaining uncertainty) and practical function (producing outputs that can be acted on). The conjecture does not address how a structurally sound system navigates this.
