## Full Text

Can retrieval-with-interpolation ever produce functional analogues of conjecture-generation as an emergent property, even if not by architectural design? The conjecture assumes a clean categorical distinction between retrieval and genuine conjecture, but this boundary may be a continuum. A system that interpolates far outside its training distribution in a structured way might be functionally indistinguishable from one generating novel conjectures. Clarifying whether the distinction is categorical or graded is necessary for the conjecture to do real work.
