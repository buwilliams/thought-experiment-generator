## Full Text

The conjecture maps onto Deutsch's 'hard to vary' criterion but claims to reveal its structural mechanism. However, 'hard to vary' was originally applied to whole explanations, not to the mutual constraint relations among parts. Does the constraint-propagation account actually explain why good explanations are hard to vary, or does it redescribe the same phenomenon at a finer grain without adding predictive content?
