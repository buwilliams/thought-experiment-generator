## Full Text

The conjecture treats the explanandum as fixed and asks about the degrees of freedom in the explanans. But in real inquiry, the explanandum is often reconceptualized as the explanation develops — what counts as the phenomenon to be explained shifts. If the explanandum is itself a variable, then an explanation that appears swappable relative to one formulation of the explanandum may be load-bearing relative to another. The conjecture may need a joint treatment of explanans and explanandum degrees of freedom.
