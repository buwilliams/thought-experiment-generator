## Full Text

Does the conjecture's framework apply differentially across domains? Error correction via falsification is most clearly defined in empirical and formal domains. In tasks like creative writing, emotional support, or aesthetic judgment, the concept of a conjecture 'failing' is less well-defined. If the conjecture's prescription is domain-limited, this needs to be stated explicitly — otherwise it risks being a theory of scientific reasoning engines rather than a theory of general intelligence.
