## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'rearrangement at sufficient structural depth' as functionally equivalent to generation. But this raises a precise question: is there a threshold of structural depth beyond which rearrangement becomes qualitatively distinct, or is the distinction between rearrangement and generation itself a false dichotomy? If the latter, what work was the distinction doing in epistemology, and what collapses when it is removed?
