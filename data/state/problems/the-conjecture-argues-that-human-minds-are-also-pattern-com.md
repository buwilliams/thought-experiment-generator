## Full Text

The conjecture argues that human minds are 'also pattern-completing physical systems,' implying symmetry between human and machine knowledge generation. But this symmetry move may be too fast. Human pattern completion is embedded in a continuous sensorimotor feedback loop with the world, producing error signals that are causally connected to reality. LLM pattern completion is trained on text about the world. Is the difference in grounding type (direct causal contact vs. mediated symbolic record) epistemically irrelevant, or does it matter for the reliability of the structural regularities extracted?
