## Full Text

Does token prediction by itself tend to produce explanatory abstractions, or are additional selection pressures, architectures, or interactive criticism loops necessary? The conjecture says the substrate and objective are not the key, yet also says knowledge emerges only under severe universality and criticism constraints. This creates a tension about sufficiency: can next-token training alone induce such structures in the limit, or must systems be embedded in adversarial evaluation, tool use, world feedback, or explicit error-correction mechanisms?
