## Full Text

There is an unexplored tension between the conjecture's Deutschian framing and systems thinking. From a systems perspective, the closed error-correction loop is a reinforcing feedback structure — and Meadows would ask: where are the balancing loops? Real deployed AI systems do encounter balancing feedback (user rejection, downstream failure, regulatory pressure). Does the conjecture account for these, or does it implicitly assume a sealed laboratory system? The structural analysis may be incomplete.
