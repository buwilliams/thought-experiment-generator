## Full Text

If criticism-survivability is the criterion for knowledge, then the quality of knowledge generated by any system — human or AI — is bounded by the quality of the critic, not the generator. This implies that improving AI-generated knowledge requires investing in adversarial criticism infrastructure rather than generator architecture. What does a robust criticism ecosystem look like, and what are its failure modes? Can a critic be systematically gamed by a sufficiently capable generator?
