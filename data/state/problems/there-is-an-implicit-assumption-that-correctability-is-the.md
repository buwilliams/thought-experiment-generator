## Full Text

There is an implicit assumption that 'correctability' is the right metric for epistemic progress. But some domains — creative generation, aesthetic judgment, strategic planning under radical uncertainty — may not have external falsification criteria in the relevant sense. Does the conjecture imply that AI systems are structurally incapable of genuine progress in these domains, or that these domains are not epistemically progressive in Deutsch's sense? This boundary condition is unexplored.
