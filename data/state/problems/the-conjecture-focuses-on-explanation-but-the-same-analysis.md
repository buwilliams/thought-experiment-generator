## Full Text

The conjecture focuses on explanation but the same analysis should apply to prediction, control, and understanding as distinct epistemic goals. A part that is swappable for explanation may be load-bearing for control (which specific lever to pull matters even if the mechanism is general). The open question is whether the conjecture generalizes across epistemic goals or is specifically about explanation, and whether the boundaries between these goals are themselves question-relative.
