## Full Text

The conjecture implicitly assumes that the criticism step can be cleanly separated from the generation step. But in human cognition, conjecture and criticism are entangled — the same cognitive apparatus that generates also partially pre-screens. If an LLM cannot perform genuine self-criticism, does this create a structural asymmetry that matters epistemically, even if the output criterion (criticism-survivability) is the same? Or does the separation of generator and critic constitute a feature rather than a deficiency?
