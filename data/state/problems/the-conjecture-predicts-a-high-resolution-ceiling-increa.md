## Full Text

The conjecture predicts a 'high-resolution ceiling' — increasing impressiveness paired with stagnant epistemic reach. This is an empirical prediction. What evidence would confirm or disconfirm it? If capability benchmarks continue to improve on genuinely novel tasks, does that falsify the conjecture, or can the conjecture always reinterpret such improvements as 'still within distribution'? The conjecture needs a falsification condition of its own.
