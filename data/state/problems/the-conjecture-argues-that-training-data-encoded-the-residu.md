## Full Text

The conjecture argues that training data encoded 'the residue of human problem-solving under selection pressure.' But selection pressure in peer review and publication is known to be severely distorted — by publication bias, replication failures, and prestige effects. If the corpus is systematically biased toward fluent-but-wrong explanations that survived social rather than epistemic filters, the inherited structure may be worse than neutral. What is the actual epistemological quality of the selection pressure LLMs absorbed?
