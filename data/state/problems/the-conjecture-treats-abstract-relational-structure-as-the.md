## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'abstract relational structure' as the key extracted quantity. But relational structure between what? Claims in text are not the same as the causal structures in the world those claims describe. A system that perfectly models the relational structure of human discourse about physics has extracted something real — but is it physics, or is it the sociology of physics knowledge? These can come apart in important ways, especially at the frontier where human discourse is confused or wrong.
