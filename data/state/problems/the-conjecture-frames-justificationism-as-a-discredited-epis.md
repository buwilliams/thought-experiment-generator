## Full Text

The conjecture frames justificationism as a discredited epistemology that the question 'inherits.' But there is a live debate about whether Popperian fallibilism fully replaces justificationism or whether some notion of epistemic support survives in a non-foundationalist form. If a moderate position is correct — that origins and generation processes are not irrelevant, only not sufficient — then the conjecture's dismissal of the 'mere rearrangement' worry may be too quick.
