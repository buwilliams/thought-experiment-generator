## Full Text

The conjecture claims load-bearing explanations have a 'unique inverse' — you can reconstruct the story from the phenomenon plus one component. This is a strong claim that maps onto the mathematical notion of an overdetermined system. Is explanatory load-bearing formally equivalent to overdetermination in a constraint satisfaction system? If so, the conjecture inherits all the machinery of that domain, including known failure modes like inconsistency under noise.
