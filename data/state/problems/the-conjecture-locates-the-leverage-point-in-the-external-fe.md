## Full Text

The conjecture locates the leverage point in the external feedback loop rather than the model. But this raises a systems-thinking question about where the boundary of 'the system' is drawn. If LLM-plus-criticism-apparatus is the relevant unit, and that unit has explanatory reach and error correction, does the conjecture's concern dissolve at the system level even if it holds at the model level? This is a stocks-and-flows question about where the epistemological work is actually being done.
