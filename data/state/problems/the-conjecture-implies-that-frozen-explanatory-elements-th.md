## Full Text

The conjecture implies that frozen explanatory elements — those calcified before the explanation was examined — are epistemically dangerous because they are invisible as assumptions. This suggests a research program: developing methods to surface inherited rigidities in active scientific explanations. What would such a method look like, and is it distinguishable from ordinary conceptual analysis or Kuhnian paradigm critique?
