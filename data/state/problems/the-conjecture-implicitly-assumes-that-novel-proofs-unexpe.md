## Full Text

The conjecture implicitly assumes that 'novel proofs, unexpected analogical transfers, synthesis across domains that produces testable predictions' are the relevant markers of new explanatory structure. But this list is drawn from a particular conception of scientific and mathematical knowledge. What about knowledge in ethics, aesthetics, or practical wisdom — domains where 'testable predictions' and 'proofs' are not the currency? The conjecture may be dissolving one narrow definition of knowledge only to smuggle in another narrow definition through its replacement criterion.
