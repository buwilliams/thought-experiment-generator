## Full Text

The conjecture frames the substrate-vs-structure debate as a local debate within an unexamined paradigm. But this framing assumes the two positions share the same foundational commitments. It is worth asking whether any existing 'structure' proposals — active learning, Bayesian approaches, causal inference architectures, or formal verification integration — already partially instantiate conjecture-criticism dynamics, and if so, why they have not resolved the problem. This would either strengthen the conjecture by showing why partial implementations fail, or weaken it by showing the problem is already being addressed.
