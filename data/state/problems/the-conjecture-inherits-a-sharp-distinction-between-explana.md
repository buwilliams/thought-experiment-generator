## Full Text

The conjecture inherits a sharp distinction between 'explanatory reach' and 'pattern matching to the surface form of explanations.' But this distinction may be harder to operationalize than it appears. What empirical test distinguishes a system with genuine explanatory reach from one that has learned to simulate the behavioral signatures of explanatory reach across a very wide distribution? If no test exists, the distinction may be doing more rhetorical than analytical work.
