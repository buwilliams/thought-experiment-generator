## Full Text

At what level of abstraction does the 'real explanation' reside when many surface details are swappable? The conjecture says the explanation is the invariant structure across substitutions, but this raises an unresolved problem of abstraction choice: which invariants are explanatory, which are too weak or generic, and how to identify the boundary where compression preserves predictive/problem-solving power rather than dissolving content into a label.
