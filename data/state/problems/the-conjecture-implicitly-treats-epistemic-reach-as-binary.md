## Full Text

The conjecture implicitly treats 'epistemic reach' as binary — either a system has it or it doesn't. But Deutsch's own account of reach is graded and domain-relative. A more precise version of this conjecture would need to specify: reach beyond what distribution, measured how, relative to which problem class? Without this, the claim risks being unfalsifiable in the same way it accuses System A of being.
