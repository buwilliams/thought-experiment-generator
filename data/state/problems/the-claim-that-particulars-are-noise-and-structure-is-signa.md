## Full Text

The claim that 'particulars are noise and structure is signal' was a design choice, not a discovery, raises the question of whether there is any non-question-relative sense in which structure is more real or more explanatory than particulars. This connects to debates in ontic structural realism. The conjecture implies a deflationary answer, but does not argue for it — and the deflationary answer has significant downstream consequences for scientific ontology.
