## Full Text

At what level of abstraction should load-bearingness be assessed? The conjecture says swappable details may indicate that the real explanation resides at a higher level, but this raises an unresolved tension: a part can be non-essential at one level and indispensable at another. The open question is how to determine the correct explanatory level, and whether 'replaceable instantiation' versus 'constitutive constraint' is an objective distinction or partly interest-relative to the problem being solved.
