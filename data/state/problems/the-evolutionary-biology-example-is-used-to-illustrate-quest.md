The evolutionary biology example is used to illustrate question-relative load-bearing: swappable on mutation identity, rigid on selective environment. But this example may cut against the conjecture — evolutionary theory is precisely the domain where mechanism successfully absorbed historical particularity (the specific lineage matters). Does this represent a third explanatory mode that is neither narrative nor mechanism as described, and does it require revising the conjecture's binary?
