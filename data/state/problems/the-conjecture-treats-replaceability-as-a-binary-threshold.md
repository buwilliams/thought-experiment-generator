## Full Text

The conjecture treats replaceability as a binary threshold (r(n) = 0 vs. r(n) > 0) when defining load-bearing nodes, but the continuous function r(n) ∈ [0,1] is never integrated into the constraint rank ρ(G). What is the correct generalization of nullity to the continuous case — perhaps a weighted nullity where each node contributes (1 - r(n)) to the rank? This matters because real explanations have nodes that are partially replaceable, and the binary treatment may produce discontinuous rigidity judgments that don't track the underlying structure.
