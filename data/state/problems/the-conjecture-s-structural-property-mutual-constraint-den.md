## Full Text

The conjecture's structural property — mutual constraint density — is defined qualitatively. Can it be operationalized? One candidate: measure the size of the substitution space for each component given the others are fixed, then take some aggregate (product, minimum, average). But this requires a metric on the space of possible component values, which may not exist for qualitative explanations. The operationalization problem may be fundamental rather than technical.
