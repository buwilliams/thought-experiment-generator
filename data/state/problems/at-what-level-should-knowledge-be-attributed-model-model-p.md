## Full Text

At what level should knowledge be attributed: model, model-plus-training-loop, or broader socio-technical system? The conjecture relocates knowledge from isolated outputs to a systemic process, but this creates an unresolved attribution problem. If the error-correcting structure partly resides in human evaluators, tools, institutions, or deployment context, it is unclear when the token-predicting system itself counts as generating knowledge rather than merely participating in a larger knowledge-producing system.
