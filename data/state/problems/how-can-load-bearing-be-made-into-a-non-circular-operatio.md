## Full Text

How can 'load-bearing' be made into a non-circular, operational criterion? The conjecture relies on counterfactual dependence, internal constraint, and 'integrity under criticism,' but these notions risk redescribing explanatory importance rather than measuring it. A key open problem is to define a structural test or metric that distinguishes genuinely constitutive parts from merely replaceable ones across cases, without smuggling in prior judgments about which explanations are better.
