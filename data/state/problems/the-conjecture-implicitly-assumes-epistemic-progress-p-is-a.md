## Full Text

The conjecture implicitly assumes epistemic progress P is a scalar or at least partially ordered quantity. But progress in different domains (mathematics, empirical science, ethics, aesthetics) may be incommensurable. Does the partial-derivative framing survive if P is vector-valued or domain-relative? Could Σ be optimal for progress in one domain while actively impeding it in another?
