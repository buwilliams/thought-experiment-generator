## Full Text

There is an implicit assumption in the conjecture that knowledge growth in AI systems should be modeled on knowledge growth in human epistemic communities. But individual human cognition is also largely reward-based and associative at the neural level — the Popperian structure emerges at the level of social and institutional criticism, not individual neurons. This raises the question of whether the conjecture is actually an argument for multi-agent architectures with adversarial institutional roles rather than a redesign of individual model training.
