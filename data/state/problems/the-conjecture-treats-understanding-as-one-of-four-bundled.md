## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'understanding' as one of four bundled requirements that can be varied independently, and concludes it is not load-bearing for knowledge-creation. But understanding in the Deutschian sense is not a psychological state — it is the possession of a good explanation. If LLMs can generate good explanations without possessing them (i.e., without the explanation being part of a coherent world-model they can deploy across contexts), then the conjecture may be granting too much by treating generation of an explanation as equivalent to its possession. The distinction between generating and possessing an explanation is unexplored.
