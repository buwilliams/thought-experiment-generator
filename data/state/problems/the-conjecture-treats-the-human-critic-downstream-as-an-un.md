## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'the human critic downstream' as an unanalyzed primitive. But criticism is itself a skill, and the quality of criticism depends on the critic's existing knowledge, tools, and incentives. If LLMs dramatically expand the surface area of claims available for criticism faster than human critical capacity scales, the conjecture-acceleration effect could be net negative — producing more low-quality conjectures than the system can evaluate, degrading the epistemic commons rather than enriching it. The conjecture does not model this failure mode.
