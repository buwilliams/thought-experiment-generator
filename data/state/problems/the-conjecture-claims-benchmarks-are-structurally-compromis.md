## Full Text

The conjecture claims benchmarks are 'structurally compromised' because they reward recall over conjecture-formation — but this is asserted rather than demonstrated. What would a benchmark that actually measures conjecture-formation capacity look like, and is it constructible? If it is not constructible in principle, the conjecture may be unfalsifiable in the same way it accuses substrate scaling of being.
