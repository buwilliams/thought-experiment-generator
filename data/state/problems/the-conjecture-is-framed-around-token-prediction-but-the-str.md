## Full Text

The conjecture is framed around token-prediction but the structural argument — that a sufficiently expressive predictor must internalize whatever causes regularities — applies to any sufficiently expressive learning system. The open question is whether the argument is architecture-neutral or whether specific architectural features of transformers (attention, positional encoding, depth) create systematic biases toward certain classes of invariants and systematic blindspots toward others, making the conjecture's conclusions architecture-specific rather than general.
