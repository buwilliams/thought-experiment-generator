## Full Text

The conjecture assumes conjecture-generation and criticism are separable pipeline stages. But in human inquiry they are deeply entangled — good conjectures are shaped by anticipation of criticism, and criticism is shaped by the conjectural framework being tested. If LLMs generate conjectures without this anticipatory structure, they may systematically produce candidates that are hard to criticize — not because they are robust, but because they are underspecified or unfalsifiable. This would mean the bottleneck diagnosis is correct but the failure mode is different from what the conjecture implies.
