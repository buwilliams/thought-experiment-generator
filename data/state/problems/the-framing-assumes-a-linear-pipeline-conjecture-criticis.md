## Full Text

The framing assumes a linear pipeline: conjecture → criticism → knowledge. But knowledge-creation systems may have important non-linear dynamics — early conjectures shape what criticism is even possible, surviving conjectures constrain the space of future conjectures, and paradigm-level structures resist criticism almost entirely. A systems thinking analysis of these feedback loops, applied specifically to LLM-augmented inquiry, is largely absent from the literature.
