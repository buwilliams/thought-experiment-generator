If the bottleneck has genuinely shifted to criticism, this predicts specific observable phenomena: increasing rates of conjecture production in LLM-adjacent fields, without corresponding increases in confirmed discoveries; growing backlogs in peer review and replication; and a widening gap between preprint volume and citation-validated knowledge. Whether these predictions are borne out empirically is a tractable research question that would either support or undermine the conjecture.
