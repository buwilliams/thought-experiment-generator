## Full Text

If most real explanations are mixed — some components load-bearing, some scaffolding — then the interesting question is whether the load-bearing components form a connected subgraph or can be isolated nodes. If they are connected, removing scaffolding should leave a coherent residual explanation. If they are not, the load-bearing parts may be individually necessary but jointly insufficient without the scaffolding. This has direct implications for explanation compression and scientific reduction that the conjecture leaves unexplored.
