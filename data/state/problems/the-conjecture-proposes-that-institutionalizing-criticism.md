## Full Text

The conjecture proposes that 'institutionalizing criticism' is the leverage point for extending LLM epistemic capacity. But in systems-thinking terms, adding a critic as an external feedback loop raises the question of loop delay, signal fidelity, and whether the feedback actually reaches the generative process in a way that changes it. If LLM weights do not update from episodic criticism, the feedback loop is open at the generative end. The structural question — what kind of feedback architecture actually closes the loop — is left unspecified and is the most actionable open problem the conjecture raises.
