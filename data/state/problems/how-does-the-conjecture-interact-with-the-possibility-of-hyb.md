How does the conjecture interact with the possibility of hybrid architectures — systems that combine retrieval components with explicit hypothesis-tracking modules, theorem provers, or active learning loops? The conjecture is framed as a binary between current retrieval systems and some future structurally sufficient system, but the design space includes many intermediate architectures. Mapping which hybrid designs would or would not cross the structural threshold would make the conjecture more useful as engineering guidance.
