## Full Text

Is there an asymmetry between domains in the ratio of genuine explanation to confabulation LLMs produce? The conjecture treats this as a general property, but it may vary dramatically — formal domains with tight logical constraints may yield higher genuine-to-confabulation ratios than open-ended interpretive domains. If so, the conjecture needs domain-relative qualification, and the design of external criticism apparatuses should be domain-specific.
