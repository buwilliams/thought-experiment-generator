## Full Text

The conjecture treats narrative and mechanism as sequential historical stages, with mechanism 'replacing' narrative. But narrative explanation persists robustly in history, law, psychiatry, and everyday cognition. The open question is whether narrative and mechanism are genuinely competing explanatory forms or whether they are answers to structurally different questions — and if the latter, the conjecture's origin story may be misdiagnosing a coexistence as a displacement.
