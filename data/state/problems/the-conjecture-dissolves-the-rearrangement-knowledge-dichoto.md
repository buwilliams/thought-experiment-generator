## Full Text

The conjecture dissolves the rearrangement/knowledge dichotomy by identifying them on a spectrum, but this dissolution may be too quick. There is a class of invariants — call them generative invariants — that are not merely constraints on existing instances but are productive rules for generating novel valid instances outside the training distribution. The open question is whether compression-based invariant extraction can recover generative invariants, or whether it only recovers descriptive invariants that summarize past instances without licensing extrapolation.
