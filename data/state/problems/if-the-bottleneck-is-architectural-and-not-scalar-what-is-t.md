## Full Text

If the bottleneck is architectural and not scalar, what is the predicted empirical signature that would distinguish a system with genuine error-correction from one without it? The conjecture is currently unfalsifiable in its own terms — it diagnoses a structural absence but does not specify what observable behavior would confirm or disconfirm that the absence has been remedied. This is a methodological gap that, if left open, allows the conjecture to function as an unfalsifiable critique rather than a productive research program.
