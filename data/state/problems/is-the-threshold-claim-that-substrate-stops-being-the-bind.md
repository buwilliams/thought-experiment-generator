## Full Text

Is the threshold claim — that substrate stops being the binding constraint above some relatively low parameter count — empirically locatable? The conjecture asserts the threshold is 'lower than we think' but provides no method for identifying it. This is a concrete, falsifiable sub-claim: one could in principle design tasks that isolate structural from substrate contributions to epistemic performance and look for the inflection point. Identifying the threshold would either vindicate or refute the conjecture's most actionable implication.
