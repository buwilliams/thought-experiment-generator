What, concretely, counts as 'participation in error-correction' for an AI system rather than for the larger human-AI system around it? The conjecture shifts the boundary from token prediction to error-correction, but leaves unresolved the individuation problem: which capacities must be internal to the model versus scaffolded by external tools, evaluators, memory, and institutions before we should say the system itself generates knowledge?
