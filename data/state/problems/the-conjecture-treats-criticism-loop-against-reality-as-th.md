## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'criticism loop against reality' as the disqualifying criterion for knowledge, but this smuggles in a specific epistemology. Under Deutschian fallibilism, criticism need not be empirical — mathematical proofs, logical refutation, and explanatory coherence all count. Does this mean LLMs with access to formal verification systems already close the loop internally? The boundary between 'conjecture' and 'knowledge' may be more permeable than the conjecture assumes.
