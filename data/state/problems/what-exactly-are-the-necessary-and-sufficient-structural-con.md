## Full Text

What exactly are the necessary and sufficient structural conditions for 'error-correcting explanation' in a token-predicting system? The conjecture says prediction is not enough and criticism/revision loops are load-bearing, but leaves open a precise criterion distinguishing genuine knowledge production from sophisticated pattern recombination. A key unresolved tension is how to operationalize explanatory reach, coherence, and resistance to refutation without collapsing back into behavioral proxies.
