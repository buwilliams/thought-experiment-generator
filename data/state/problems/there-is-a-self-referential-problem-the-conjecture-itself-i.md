## Full Text

There is a self-referential problem: the conjecture itself is a product of some (φ, κ, π) operating on some substrate. If the conjecture is correct that current AI systems cannot escape their training distribution's convex hull, then an AI system cannot reliably evaluate whether this conjecture is true or false — it would be systematically blind to the error class the conjecture identifies. This is either a fatal epistemic limitation of using AI to reason about AI epistemics, or it points toward a meta-level criterion for what genuine criticism of AI reasoning would require.
