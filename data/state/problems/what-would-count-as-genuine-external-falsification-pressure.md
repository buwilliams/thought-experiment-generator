## Full Text

What would count as 'genuine external falsification pressure' in practice? The conjecture draws a sharp line between internal and external criteria, but this boundary may be unstable: any external criterion, once incorporated into training, becomes internal. Is there a principled account of what makes a falsification source permanently external, or does every external tribunal eventually get absorbed into the distribution? This is the implementation gap at the heart of the conjecture, and it is unresolved.
