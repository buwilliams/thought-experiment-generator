## Full Text

The conjecture offloads the knowledge-creating step to 'the human critic downstream,' but this assumes the critic is epistemically independent of the LLM output. If the critic's attention, framing, and hypothesis space are systematically shaped by the LLM's confabulations, then the criticism is not independent — it is downstream of the model's prior. The conjecture-criticism loop may be corrupted at the input stage. This is a structural feedback problem that the conjecture ignores.
