The conjecture applies Deutschian criteria (hard-to-vary, genuine reach) to evaluate outputs. But these criteria were developed for scientific explanations about physical reality. Do they transfer cleanly to domains where the generative mechanisms are social, mathematical, or normative rather than causal-physical? Or does the conjecture implicitly privilege a particular domain of knowledge where the criteria are most tractable?
