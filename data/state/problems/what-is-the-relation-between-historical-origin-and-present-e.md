## Full Text

What is the relation between historical origin and present explanatory necessity? The conjecture ties swappability to inherited narrative, pedagogical, or rhetorical residues, but many features may originate in those contexts and later become genuinely indispensable, while some criticism-forged details may become obsolete. The unresolved tension is whether genealogy is evidentially relevant or merely suggestive, and how to distinguish origin from current functional role in an explanation.
