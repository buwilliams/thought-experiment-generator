## Full Text

The conjecture identifies 'LLM-plus-critic' as the productive epistemic unit, but leaves the boundary of that system undefined. If the critic is constitutive of the knowledge-creation event, then the LLM alone never creates knowledge — the system does. This is not a minor terminological point: it determines whether LLMs have epistemic standing or are merely components in a larger epistemic machine. The question of where to draw the system boundary is unresolved and has direct implications for how we assign epistemic credit and design AI architectures.
