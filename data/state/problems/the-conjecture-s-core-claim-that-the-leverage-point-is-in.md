## Full Text

The conjecture's core claim — that the leverage point is in feedback loop organization — is a systems-thinking claim about where to intervene. But it does not specify what the feedback loop should be connected to. Criticism of what, by whom, according to what standard? Without specifying the target of the feedback, 'organize feedback loops to destroy bad ideas' is a structural description without a structural specification.
