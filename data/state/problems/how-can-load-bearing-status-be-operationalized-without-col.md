How can 'load-bearing' status be operationalized without collapsing into circularity? The conjecture defines load-bearing parts by the exclusion work they do under criticism, but it remains open how to identify and measure that work independently of already judging the explanation good. A new problem is to develop non-circular criteria or procedures for testing whether a component genuinely rules out alternatives, changes derivations, or alters counterfactual structure.
