## Full Text

The conjecture treats the critical tradition as an external corrective mechanism, but does not examine who controls the feedback that reaches the system's developers. If the critical tradition's outputs (corrections, identified errors, refutations) do not flow back into training in a systematic way, then the system is epistemically decoupled from the tradition it nominally participates in. This raises the question of whether there is a meaningful sense in which the system 'enters' a critical tradition at all, or whether it merely produces inputs to one while remaining structurally isolated from its outputs.
