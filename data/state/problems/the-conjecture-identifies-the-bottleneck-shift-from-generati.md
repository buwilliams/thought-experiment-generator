## Full Text

The conjecture identifies the bottleneck shift from generation to evaluation, then claims the leverage point is 'better tools for falsification.' But this is underspecified. What would a falsification tool look like for the kinds of conjectures LLMs produce — natural language claims, informal arguments, speculative hypotheses? The conjecture gestures at a systems-level intervention without modeling the feedback structure of the evaluation subsystem. The leverage claim is asserted, not derived.
