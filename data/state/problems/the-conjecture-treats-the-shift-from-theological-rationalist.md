## Full Text

The conjecture treats the shift from theological/rationalist to empiricist/individuated knowledge as a historical contingency that could have gone otherwise. But there may be a structural reason why knowledge became process-dependent: the need to assign epistemic responsibility and to distinguish reliable from unreliable sources. If LLM outputs are accepted as knowledge without a responsible knowing subject, what replaces the accountability function that process-dependence was implicitly serving? This is not just a philosophical question — it has direct implications for how institutions should treat LLM-generated claims.
