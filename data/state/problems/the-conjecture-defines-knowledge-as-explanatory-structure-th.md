## Full Text

The conjecture defines knowledge as explanatory structure that tracks reality, but does not address what 'tracking reality' requires for abstract domains — mathematics, philosophy, formal systems. In these domains, criticism loops do not involve empirical contact. This creates an unresolved tension: either the Deutschian criterion applies differently across domains, or the conjecture's scope is narrower than it presents itself to be.
