## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'destroying bad ideas rapidly' as the target behavior, but error-correction requires a prior criterion for what counts as an error. In a system without a fixed ground truth, who or what supplies that criterion? This is the demarcation problem applied to AI architecture — and the conjecture does not address it, yet the entire structural proposal depends on resolving it.
