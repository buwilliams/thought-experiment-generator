## Full Text

The conjecture is framed around LLMs specifically, but the structural argument — that a system compressing existing conjectural space cannot escape that space — applies to any bounded knowledge system, including individual human minds. A human trained only on existing knowledge faces the same asymptotic limit. The conjecture may be identifying a property of all finite epistemic agents, not a distinctive property of LLMs. If so, the boundary it claims to reveal is not between LLMs and humans but between any closed system and genuine novelty.
