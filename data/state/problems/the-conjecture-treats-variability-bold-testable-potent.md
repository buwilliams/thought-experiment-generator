## Full Text

The conjecture treats 'variability' — bold, testable, potentially wrong conjectures — as epistemically valuable and claims reward optimization suppresses it. But there is a known tension here: high variability in outputs is also the source of hallucination, confabulation, and incoherence. The conjecture needs an account of how to distinguish epistemically productive variability from noise. Without this, the prescription to increase conjecture-space exploration could worsen the very failure modes the field has been trying to address.
