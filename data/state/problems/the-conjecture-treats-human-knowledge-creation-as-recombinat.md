## Full Text

The conjecture treats human knowledge-creation as recombination to defuse the objection that LLMs 'merely recombine.' But this move is too fast: the Deutschian account of creativity holds that genuine conjecture involves a leap that is not reducible to prior structure, even if it uses prior materials. If that irreducibility is real, then the question of whether LLM recombination ever achieves it is not settled by pointing out that humans also recombine — it requires a positive account of what the threshold is and how to detect when it has been crossed. The conjecture gestures at this with 'genuine conjunction' but does not define the threshold.
