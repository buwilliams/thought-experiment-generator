## Full Text

What is the non-circular criterion for the proposed 'boundary' between rearrangement and genuine knowledge? The conjecture appeals to compression, unification, portability, and survival under criticism across indefinitely many contexts, but it leaves open how to define or test this without presupposing the very notion of explanation it aims to explain. A new problem is to formulate a principled operational discriminator: what observable capacities, failure modes, or structural invariants would distinguish error-correcting explanation from high-order interpolation in finite systems?
