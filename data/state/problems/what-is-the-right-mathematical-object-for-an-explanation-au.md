## Full Text

What is the right mathematical object for an explanation: automorphism group, causal model, proof structure, category, or something else? The conjecture treats explanations as structured maps with symmetries, but it is unresolved whether ordinary scientific and mathematical explanations actually admit a representation where load-bearingness is captured by automorphisms rather than by intervention sensitivity, counterfactual dependence, or proof-theoretic indispensability.
