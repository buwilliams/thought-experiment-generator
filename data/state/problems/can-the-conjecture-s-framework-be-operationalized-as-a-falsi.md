## Full Text

Can the conjecture's framework be operationalized as a falsifiable research program? Specifically: if we construct an external criticism apparatus and apply it to LLM outputs across domains, what result would confirm or disconfirm the conjecture? The claim that LLMs produce genuine knowledge 'accidentally' needs a test condition — otherwise it risks being unfalsifiable and thus outside the Popperian framework it invokes.
