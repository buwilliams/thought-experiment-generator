## Full Text

If revisability is the criterion, then the conjecture implies that a frozen LLM (weights fixed, no fine-tuning, no world feedback) is epistemically outside the loop regardless of output quality. But this seems to prove too much — a published book is also frozen, yet we consider it a vehicle for knowledge. The conjecture needs to distinguish between a system being in the loop versus a system's outputs being usable by agents who are in the loop. This distinction may rehabilitate frozen LLMs but requires a more careful account of where knowledge resides.
