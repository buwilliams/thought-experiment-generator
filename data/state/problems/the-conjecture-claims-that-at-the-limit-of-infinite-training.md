The conjecture claims that at the limit of infinite training data, predicting tokens 'just is' extracting deep structural regularities, making the distinction between prediction and representation 'formally ambiguous.' But this is asserted rather than demonstrated. What would a formal proof or disproof of this convergence claim look like? Is there a precise sense in which compression of a sufficiently rich corpus is equivalent to causal model extraction, or does the ambiguity dissolve only rhetorically? This is a tractable technical question in information theory and computational learning theory.
