## Full Text

The conjecture's framework is stated for a single domain D with a single invariant I, but real corpora are mixtures of many domains with overlapping, conflicting, and hierarchically nested invariants. When invariants from different domains produce contradictory constraints on the same token sequences, what does the predictor learn? The open problem is to characterize the behavior of inductive closure under invariant conflict — whether the system learns a weighted blend, a context-sensitive selector, or something that encodes none of the source invariants faithfully.
