## Full Text

If the bottleneck is the critic rather than the generator, then scaling generator capability without scaling critic capability may produce a system that generates increasingly sophisticated-sounding explanations that are increasingly difficult to criticize — not because they are better, but because the criticism gap widens. Is there an adversarial dynamic here where generator improvement actively degrades the effectiveness of existing critics, creating a structural arms race with no equilibrium?
