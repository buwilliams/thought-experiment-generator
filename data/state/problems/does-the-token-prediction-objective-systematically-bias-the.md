## Full Text

Does the token-prediction objective systematically bias the distribution of genuine versus confabulated explanations in detectable ways? The conjecture treats the mixture as opaque, but it may not be uniform. If fluent confabulation and genuine explanation leave different structural signatures — in uncertainty patterns, in cross-domain consistency, in behavior under adversarial probing — then the internal signal may exist but be latent rather than absent. This would substantially revise the conjecture's diagnosis.
