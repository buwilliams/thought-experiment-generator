## Full Text

Does confident confabulation actually scale monotonically with capacity, as the conjecture claims? This is stated as evidence but is itself a contested empirical question. Larger models do show improved calibration on some benchmarks, and chain-of-thought prompting partially recovers uncertainty-tracking. If confabulation does not scale monotonically, the conjecture's boundary-case argument weakens significantly. The relationship between scale and epistemic failure mode deserves its own careful treatment.
