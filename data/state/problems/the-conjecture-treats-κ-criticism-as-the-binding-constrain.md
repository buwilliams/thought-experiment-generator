The conjecture treats κ (criticism) as the binding constraint, but φ (conjecture formation) and κ are not independent — the space of conjectures a system generates is shaped by what it has learned to expect criticism to reward. This creates a co-evolutionary dynamic between φ and κ that the static partial-derivative framing may obscure. What is the correct dynamical systems model for their interaction, and does the bottleneck shift depending on which is updated first?
