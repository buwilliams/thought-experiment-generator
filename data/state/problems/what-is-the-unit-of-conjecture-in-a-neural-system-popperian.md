## Full Text

What is the unit of conjecture in a neural system? Popperian epistemology operates on propositions that have truth conditions and can be falsified by evidence. Neural network outputs are not obviously propositional in this sense — they are distributions over tokens, or vectors in embedding space. Before the conjecture's prescription can be operationalized, there must be an account of what it means for a sub-symbolic system to 'form a conjecture.' This is a foundational translation problem that the conjecture skips over.
