## Full Text

How should explanatory value trade off between abstraction and discriminatory power? The conjecture treats swappability as signaling either deeper invariant structure or emptiness, but leaves open how to evaluate explanations that gain compression and generality while losing local specificity, or vice versa. A key unresolved tension is whether there is a principled frontier between fruitful abstraction and underconstrained vacuity.
