## Full Text

Is productive explanatory power always associated with brittleness and compression, or do some domains require redundancy, multiple realizability, or audience-relative scaffolding as part of the explanation itself? The conjecture risks overvaluing minimal exclusion structures and undervaluing explanatory forms in biology, social systems, law, or education where robustness, abstraction, and interchangeable representations may be essential rather than ornamental. The open question is where the load-bearing/swappable distinction genuinely tracks truth-seeking versus where it misclassifies legitimate explanatory pluralism.
