## Full Text

The conjecture's Meadows-inspired framing identifies feedback loops as the leverage point. But Meadows also identifies that changing the goal of a system — what the feedback loop is trying to achieve — is higher leverage than changing the feedback loop structure itself. Is there a level above Σ in this framework — call it the 'epistemic goal function' — and if so, what determines it and how could it be changed?
