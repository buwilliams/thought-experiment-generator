## Full Text

The conjecture focuses on outputs entering a critical tradition, but does not address the feedback loop back to the generator. A human who receives criticism can update their internal model; the token-prediction system (in its current form) cannot update from individual interactions. This asymmetry may be epistemically significant not because of the mechanism of generation but because of the capacity for self-correction over time. Does the conjecture need to distinguish between one-shot conjecture production and the iterative refinement that characterizes genuine inquiry?
