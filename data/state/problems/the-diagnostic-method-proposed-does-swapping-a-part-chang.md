## Full Text

The diagnostic method proposed — 'does swapping a part change what is being explained, or only who is doing the explaining?' — presupposes we can individuate the explanandum independently of the explanation. But in many cases the explanandum is partly constituted by the explanation (e.g., what counts as 'the same disease' shifts with mechanistic understanding). This circularity may not be vicious, but it needs explicit treatment.
