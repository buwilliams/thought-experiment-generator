## Full Text

The conjecture maps explanation structure onto a spectrum from logical necessity (proof) to pure narrative (confabulation). But mathematical proofs are themselves embedded in axiomatic choices that are not uniquely determined — you can swap axioms and preserve proof structure. Does this mean proofs are load-bearing locally but swappable globally? If so, the conjecture needs a level-relative formulation, and the question becomes: at what level of description does constraint actually operate?
