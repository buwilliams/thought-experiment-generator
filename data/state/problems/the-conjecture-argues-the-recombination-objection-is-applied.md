## Full Text

The conjecture argues the recombination objection is applied asymmetrically to LLMs versus humans. But this may concede too much too fast. The relevant disanalogy might not be recombination per se, but the absence of a generative model of why a combination should be true — humans conjecture from causal intuitions, LLMs from distributional patterns. Whether this distinction is principled or merely a heuristic is itself an open and important question.
