## Full Text

The conjecture treats mutual constraint density as a binary threshold, but it may be a continuous spectrum with no natural threshold. What determines where the threshold lies — is it a function of the number of valid substitutes per component, the product of those numbers, or something else? Without a principled threshold criterion, the load-bearing/non-load-bearing distinction may itself be underdetermined in the very sense the conjecture diagnoses.
