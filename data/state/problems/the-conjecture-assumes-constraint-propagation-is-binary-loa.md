## Full Text

The conjecture assumes constraint propagation is binary (load-bearing vs. decorative), but real explanations may have graded or hierarchical constraint structures — some parts constrain many others, some constrain few. Is there a topology of constraint graphs that would let us rank explanatory elements by their structural centrality, and does this topology predict anything about where explanations fail?
