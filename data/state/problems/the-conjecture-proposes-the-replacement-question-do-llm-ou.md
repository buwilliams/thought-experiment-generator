## Full Text

The conjecture proposes the replacement question: 'Do LLM outputs introduce new explanatory structure that survives criticism?' But 'survives criticism' is doing enormous work here. Criticism by whom, using what standards, over what timescale? If an LLM produces a novel proof that no human checks for a decade, does it constitute knowledge in the interim? This exposes a latent tension between the objective-content view of knowledge and the social-epistemic infrastructure required to operationalize 'criticism' — a tension the conjecture inherits from Popper but doesn't resolve.
