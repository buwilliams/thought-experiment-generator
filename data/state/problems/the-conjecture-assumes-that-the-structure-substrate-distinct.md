## Full Text

The conjecture assumes that the structure-substrate distinction is stable across different problem domains. But some domains (e.g., formal mathematics) may be ones where pattern-matching at scale genuinely approximates conjecture-testing, while others (e.g., novel causal inference) may not. Is the conjecture domain-general, or does it require a taxonomy of problem types to specify where structure is load-bearing and where substrate is sufficient?
