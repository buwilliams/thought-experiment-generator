## Full Text

The conjecture treats the explanation graph as static, but explanations evolve as new inferential connections are discovered or old ones are severed. What is the dynamics of constraint rank under graph modification? Specifically, is there a phase transition — a critical point at which adding one new inferential edge causes nullity to drop to zero? If so, this would identify a precise structural moment at which an explanation becomes rigid, which could be empirically tracked in the history of scientific theories.
