## Full Text

The conjecture focuses on LLMs as the epistemic agent of interest, but the 'LLM-plus-critic' framing raises the question of whether the same analysis applies to other non-conscious generative systems — formal proof assistants, evolutionary algorithms, stochastic search processes. If the argument works for LLMs, it should generalize, and if it generalizes fully, the conjecture may prove too much: it may imply that any process producing valid novel outputs creates knowledge, which dissolves the concept rather than refining it. Exploring the boundary conditions of the argument would sharpen it considerably.
