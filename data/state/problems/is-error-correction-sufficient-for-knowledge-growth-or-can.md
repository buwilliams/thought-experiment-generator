## Full Text

Is error-correction sufficient for knowledge growth, or can a system participate in criticism and revision while still only optimizing socially reinforced correlations rather than truth-tracking explanations? The conjecture treats uncriticized output as the key opposite of knowledge, but an unresolved tension is whether feedback loops themselves can be epistemically corrupt, causing systems to become better at passing criticism rituals without producing deeper understanding.
