It is objective because once those conditions are in place, a wide class of predictive tasks—thermodynamic, causal, informational, biological—converge on the same directional structure. The universe is not merely permitting an arrow; its state distribution forces predictors to discover one.

So the illuminating synthesis is: **time has a direction because reversibility in the laws does not imply reversibility in the distribution of states.** The true invariant is not in the equations alone but in the combination of laws, boundary conditions, and the generalization pressures placed on any system trying to model the world.
