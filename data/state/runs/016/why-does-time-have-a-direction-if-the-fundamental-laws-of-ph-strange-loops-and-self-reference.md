## Conjecture

**Conjecture:**  
Time’s direction is not imposed by the fundamental laws but *generated when a time-symmetric world contains self-referential subsystems that must model their own persistence across changing states*. The “arrow of time” appears where physics folds back on itself through memory, records, prediction, and identity — in other words, through a strange loop.

Here is the collision of the perspective with the problem:

If the base laws are time-symmetric, then microscopic evolution alone does not privilege past over future. But a conscious or information-processing system is not just another evolving state; it is a system that represents other states, including earlier versions of itself, and uses those representations to maintain continuity. That creates a hierarchy: physical state → encoded record → model of state → model of the modeler. When this hierarchy closes, the system becomes temporally self-referential. It can say, in effect, “I am now the successor of that earlier me.”

This loop is asymmetric in practice even if the laws are not. Why? Because self-reference requires *records*, and records are not neutral under time reversal. A record is a present structure that points to a constrained subset of prior states. The future, by contrast, is not present as record but as branching possibility. So once a system internally distinguishes “what is encoded” from “what is merely possible,” it generates an intrinsic orientation: the recorded side is called past; the open side is called future.

On this view, the thermodynamic arrow matters not merely because entropy increases, but because entropy increase enables stable record-production. Low-entropy conditions permit the formation of reliable memories, fossils, traces, and observers. These traces then feed back into self-modeling systems, which organize their behavior around the asymmetry between recoverable information and unrecoverable possibility. The arrow of time is therefore not just statistical; it is *epistemic and loop-structural*. It arises where the universe contains entities for whom some states function as evidence and others as anticipation.

What follows is that temporal direction is emergent in the same sense consciousness is emergent: not absent from the base rules, but not readable off them without considering recursive organization. The laws permit both directions; strange loops select one by building identities out of memory and action. A being without self-referential memory might inhabit a time-symmetric world without a lived arrow. A being with such loops cannot.

So the illumination is this: the “direction of time” is the phenomenological and functional signature of self-reference embedded in thermodynamic history. Physics supplies reversible dynamics; strange loops turn reversible change into irreversible *aboutness* — a world with before/after, cause/record, self/persistence.
