So the illuminating claim is this: the universe’s exceptional beginning is exceptional in exactly the way any world must be if it is to host open-ended information transformation under selection. The deep puzzle then becomes not why the universe was special *for us*, but why reality admits boundary conditions from which computation can bootstrap itself into complexity, irreversibility, and knowledge at all.
