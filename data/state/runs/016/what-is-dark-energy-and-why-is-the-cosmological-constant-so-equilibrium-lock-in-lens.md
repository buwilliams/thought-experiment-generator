So the perspective distinguishes two possibilities. If we are merely confused, better local calculations should close the gap. One needs a theory in which gravity does not passively accept every vacuum contribution as a source term, but instead incorporates a **global constraint, symmetry, or feedback loop** that makes most vacuum energy physically non-operative.
