## Conjecture

**Conjecture:** Intentionality arises not at the center of ordinary physical functioning, but at the **boundary condition where a physical system must keep correlating with what is absent, distant, future, or merely possible in order to persist**. Aboutness appears when mere causal interaction is pushed to the limit of **error-sensitive self-maintenance across counterfactual variation**.

Here is the collision between the perspective and the problem:

If we look at ordinary matter in the middle range, physical processes seem not to be “about” anything. A rock rolls downhill; a molecule binds; a circuit switches. These are just causal transitions. So push to the extremes.

At one extreme, imagine a system with **zero internal memory, zero modeling, zero capacity to distinguish better from worse futures**. It can still be causally affected, but nothing in it is about anything. It is only driven.

At the other extreme, imagine a system that must survive in an environment by acting not only on what is immediately present, but on **latent structure**: food behind obstacles, predators not yet visible, seasons not yet arrived, social intentions not directly observable. To remain viable, it must preserve internal states that stand in for external realities across delay, noise, and possible error. Those internal states are not mere effects; they are **usable surrogates** whose success depends on whether the world is as they indicate.

That is the boundary where intentionality appears.

The key step is this: **aboutness is what causal states become when they are embedded in a system whose continued existence depends on the correction of representational error**. A state counts as about X when:
1. it is produced and used within a larger control system,
2. the system’s success depends on treating it as a guide to X,
3. there is a real distinction between correct and incorrect use,
4. that distinction matters for persistence.

So intentionality is not added to physics from outside. It is a structural feature that emerges in certain physical systems when causation is organized into **feedback loops that traffic in counterfactuals**: if the prey were there, this state would guide pursuit; if not, it would mislead; and the organism must be able, directly or evolutionarily, to be selected against for such error.

Pushed further, this suggests that intentionality is strongest where reference is least tied to immediate stimulus: imagination, planning, mathematics, fiction. These are not exceptions but intensifications. They reveal that aboutness is fundamentally not simple correlation, but **error-correctable reach beyond the present**.

So the conjecture is: **intentionality is the limit phenomenon of physical control systems that must encode and act on absent structure under conditions where misrepresentation is possible and consequential**. At the edge where survival depends on getting non-present reality right, physics becomes semantics.
